About 12plums

12plums is a subscription box service that offers all-natural beauty, personal care and lifestyle products to expectant and new moms and their newborns.

When we are pregnant, we adjust our routines to stop drinking, smoking and eating certain foods because of the proven health hazards to our unborn children. Furthermore, 60% of what we put on our skin enters our bloodstream exposing our fetuses to a range of unsafe chemicals. Imagine how much is transferred through breastfeeding. Nonetheless, very few women change their beauty routines to eliminate ingredients that are potentially hazardous. This is probably a combination of willful blindness and a lack of education as to the wealth of safe and effective products on the market.

Our mission is to help mothers pamper themselves and their newborns safely and without compromise. All beauty should be safe and luxurious. That is our motto. To that end, we partner with the best all-natural and organic products to introduce to our subscribers in our monthly boxes.
