FAQs

What is 12plums?
12plums is a subscription box service that offers all-natural beauty and personal care products to expectant and new moms. When we are pregnant we adjust our routines to stop drinking, smoking and eating certain foods because of the proven health hazards to our unborn children. Nonetheless, very few women change their beauty routines to eliminate ingredients that are also potentially hazardous. This is probably a combination of willful blindness and lack of education as to the wealth of safe and effective products on the market. Our mission is to help mothers pamper themselves and their newborns safely and without the compromise many wrongfully associate with "natural beauty". To that end, we are partnering with the best all-natural products which we will introduce to our subscribers in our boxes. This will allow our subscribers to learn about and test products such as yours that they might not otherwise have known about.
Who is 12plums for?
12plums is all about moms. If you are a “green mommy”, then 12plums is for you. If you have never used organic or all-natural products and think they aren’t any good, the 12plums is for you. If you fall somewhere in between, guess what, 12plums is for you too. As long as you are pregnant, or a new mother, you fit our profile. We bring you the best beauty and personal care products to pamper yourself and your newborn safely and beautifully. All beauty should be safe and luxurious and that is what 12plums is all about.
What are your standards for the products included in each 12plums box?
We evaluate all of our brands thoroughly to make sure that they animal and eco-friendly, sustainable, free of harsh chemicals, synthetic dyes, artificial fragrances and colors, parabens, phthalates, sulfates, petroleum, mineral oils, retinoids, salicylic acids, BHAs, BHTs, soy (except for some of our candles), certain essential oils, and the list goes on and on. Our goal is to provide expectant and new mothers with personal care products that are truly safe for mother and child. When there is a difference of opinion on an ingredient, we err on the side of caution. Many but not all of our brands are organic but even the organic ingredients are thoroughly vetted. We test all of products and evaluate their ingredients no matter what. Nonetheless, we recommend that you look over the ingredients for yourself and consult your physician if you have any questions. You have the final say as to what you put on yourself and your child. We just want to make it easier for you. However, you may have allergies or other reactions that we cannot protect against. Furthermore, 12plums does not make any guarantees as to the safety to you or your child as related to these products but we do our best to provide you with only the safest products on the market.

I requested an invite to12plums, does this mean I have an account?
No. Requesting an invite does not automatically create your account. Once your are invited and subscribe to 12plums, you will create your account when prompted during checkout.
Is 12plums available outside the US?
No. We plan on expanding to Canada, France and the UK. Check back in soon.
How do I fill out my personal profile?
You will be prompted to fill out your profile as soon as you create a 12plums Account. You can sign into your account at anytime to update your information. This profile is a short questionnaire regarding your prenatal or postpartum stage and your beauty/grooming preferences. It helps us select products that are appropriate for your stage of pregnancy and your personal preferences. To edit or update your profile, click on "Account Settings" in your account, and then click on "Edit Profile". Fill out the questionnaire and then save your preferences.
Can I select my samples each month?
No. Boxes always contain 4-5 samples from a mix of different categories (skincare, babycare, haircare, fragrance, lifestyle, etc.). There is no option to curate your own boxes however, we do our best to select products for you based on your stage of pregnancy and personal preferences. Part of the fun of our box is discovering and experimenting with new and different products. To receive samples suited to you, please fill out your profile.
Are there any hidden fees?
No, you pay only the subscription fee. There are no additional shipping or handling fees for the monthly 12plums box. However, shipping and handling may apply when you are purchasing items from our e-commerce site. Please note that in NY, NJ, MA, TN, and VA sales tax may apply.
When can I expect my first shipment?
Our boxes ship mid-month. Check your order confirmation for an estimated shipping date. Our boxes ship from New York and should arrive an estimated 5-10 business days after they ship. You will receive an email with a tracking number so you can follow your box's progress.
When will I be billed for the monthly recurring membership?
When you place your order for the monthly membership, you will be billed for your first box immediately. After that, you will be billed at the beginning of each month.
How do I update my billing and/or shipping information?
Simply log into your account at 12plums.com and click on your "My Account" page. You will then see links to update your billing and shipping information. If you have any problems, send us an email at info@12plums.com.
Can I give a 12plums membership as a gift?
Of course! 12plums is a great gift to give to your newly pregnant wife, daughter, sister, friend and/or as baby shower gift. We offer 3, 6, 9 or 12 month memberships to be given as gifts. You simply purchase a gift membership and make a one-time payment. The recipient will be contacted to set up their shipping address and personal profile.
If I have a gift subscription, what happens when I reach my last month?
If you have a gift subscription, it will be terminated after you receive your last box. After your last box is received, you can re-subscribe and become a regular recurring subscriber. Just login to your 12plums account and go to the ‘re-up’ tab to re-subscribe.
What is your cancellation policy?
Monthly members can cancel their 12plums subscription at any time. There are no cancellation fees. Simply go to your Account page and click on the 'Cancel Subscription' link. You can also contact us at info@12plums.com to cancel. If you would like to cancel your monthly subscription, make sure to send in your cancellation request before 12am EST the day immediately proceeding your rebilling, otherwise you will be billed for and sent the following 12plums box.
If you have a bought a gift or pre-paid subscription (3, 6, 9, or 12 months), your subscription will end automatically after the last box is shipped. You may not end it early and obtain a refund.
Where do I buy the full-size version of a sample?
You can buy full-size versions of your sample in the 12plums shop. If we do not carry it, we provide you with a link to another shop that carries it.
I'm a woman of color. Will 12plums fit my needs?
We sure how so as we have women of all colors on our team. We try our best to only send you products that fit your beauty/grooming needs, skin color, and hair type. Fill out your profile to ensure that we can pick samples that will work for you.
I have allergies to certain products and ingredients. What do I do?
Unfortunately, we are not able to guarantee that your box won’t cause allergic reactions. If you do have known allergies to certain products/ingredients, we highly suggest that you first check the product's ingredients before you test out your sample. Always contact your physician if you have any questions.
